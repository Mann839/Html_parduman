Welcome to Jaipur

Jaipur, the capital of Rajasthan, is known as the Pink City due to the distinct color of its buildings. It is famous for its rich history, stunning architecture, and vibrant culture. Key attractions include the Hawa Mahal, City Palace, Amber Fort, and Jantar Mantar. Jaipur is also a hub for traditional Rajasthani cuisine and handicrafts.
